Santani Wellness Resort (Kandy)

Santani Wellness Resort (Kandy): Nestled in the hills, Santani provides holistic wellness experiences focused on Ayurvedic treatments, yoga, and meditation. It offers tailor-made programs for detox, stress relief, and lifestyle improvement, making it an ideal destination to disconnect from the world​

Barberyn Beach Ayurveda Resort (Weligama)

Barberyn Beach Ayurveda Resort (Weligama): Known for its authentic Ayurvedic therapies, Barberyn Beach provides a relaxing coastal retreat. Guests can enjoy healthy, Ayurvedic cuisine while receiving personalized treatments to balance both body and mind

Aarunya Nature Resort (Matale)

Aarunya Nature Resort (Matale): This eco-friendly resort offers yoga retreats, Ayurvedic spa treatments, and meditation sessions, all set against the backdrop of Sri Lanka's stunning natural landscapes. It's perfect for those looking to unwind and embrace sustainable living.​

Sen Wellness Sanctuary (Tangalle)

Sen Wellness Sanctuary (Tangalle): Featuring a design inspired by nature, this sanctuary offers a combination of yoga, meditation, and Ayurvedic treatments. With an emphasis on detox and relaxation, it's a tranquil spot for a longer wellness retreat.​
