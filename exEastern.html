Eastern Coast

Sri Lanka, Asia

The Eastern Coast of Sri Lanka offers a unique blend of serene beaches, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant local life. Trincomalee, one of the key highlights, is known for its stunning beaches, including Nilaveli and Uppuveli, and its historical significance, with sites like the Koneswaram Temple and the ancient Fort Frederick. The region's natural harbor is among the deepest in the world and provides a picturesque backdrop.

Arugam Bay, another prominent spot, is celebrated for its excellent surfing conditions and laid-back atmosphere, attracting surfers and beach lovers alike. The bay has a relaxed vibe with a range of accommodations and eateries that cater to both locals and tourists.

Pasikudah is renowned for its calm, shallow waters, making it ideal for swimming and family-friendly beach activities. The area has been developing steadily, offering more amenities and services while preserving its natural charm.

Overall, the Eastern Coast combines beautiful coastal scenery with opportunities for water sports, relaxation, and exploration of local culture and history.
