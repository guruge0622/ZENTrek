Payment Processing



ZENTrek provides flexible and secure payment options to cater to our global customers. Choose the method that best suits your convenience and complete your booking seamlessly.



PayPal

Make instant and secure payments using your PayPal account. It supports multiple currencies, making it a convenient choice for international customers.

Online Payment Gateway

Use your credit or debit card to complete the payment through our secure online payment gateway. All transactions are encrypted to ensure your safety.

Bank Transfers

Easily transfer funds from your local bank to our Sri Lankan bank account. We support transfers from all major international banks. Bank details will be shared during the booking process.

Other Payment Methods

We also accept additional payment methods such as mobile payment apps and regional payment platforms. Contact us to explore all available options.




If you need assistance with the payment process or have special requirements, feel free to contact us. Our team is here to guide you through every step!
